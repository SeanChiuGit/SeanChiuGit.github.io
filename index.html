Basket Hoop

HTML, JavaScript, CSS

A creative entry for the GMTK Game Jam 2023 (theme: “Roles Reversed”), co-developed by Swedenfish and me. In 2025, we spontaneously challenged ourselves to build a game around the 2023 theme within 48 hours— not as part of the original jam, but as a fun personal project.

Instead of controlling the player shooting the ball, you are the hoop itself! Position yourself to catch as many basketballs as possible.

This is just the beginning — we’ll keep refining the game and adding new content step by step!
